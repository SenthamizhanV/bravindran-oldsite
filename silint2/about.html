Professor V. Kamakoti (Founder-Director) is currently Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, and Associate Dean of Industrial Consultancy and Sponsored Research ( ICSR ) at IIT Madras. He also serves as a Director in charge of technical operations of City Union Bank.

Prof Kamakoti is Member- NSAB, National Security Council. He served as Chairman – AI Task force for Ministry of Commerce and Industries.

He is the PI for SHAKTHI ( Open Source Processor Development System ), RISE - Reconfigurable and Intelligent Systems Engineering (RISE) lab, C- DISHA - Pratap Subrahmanyam Centre for Digital Intelligence and Secure Hardware Architecture. Professor Kamakoti, his team have been leading the efforts of design and development of indigenous, India specific family of Micro-Controllers based on RISC-V Open Instruction Set Architecture. He coordinates the security and defence domain of the IMPRINT Program and is one of the Program committee members of Deity Sponsored NMDP.

Prof Kamakoti has been awarded Abdul Kalam Technology Innovation National Fellowship in 2020, IESA Techno Visionary Award in 2018, IBM Faculty Award in 2016, DRDO Academy Excellence Award in 2013 by Honourable Prime Minister Shri.Narendra Modi and in 2007 was awarded the "Young Faculty Recognition Award" by IIT Madras.

Professor B. Ravindran (Founder-Director) heads the Robert Bosch Centre for Data Science & Artificial Intelligence (RBC-DSAI) at IIT Madras, the leading interdisciplinary AI research centre in India. He is the Mindtree Faculty Fellow and Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Madras. He has held visiting positions at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India and University of Technology Sydney, Australia. He is the No.1 Deep Reinforcement Learning Expert and among top 3 Machine Learning Experts in India. He has been recognized, in 2020, as a senior member of the Association for Advancement of AI (AAAI) for his long-standing contributions to AI.

He received his PhD from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst and his Master’s degree from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. He has more than two decades of research experience in AI, ML and specifically Reinforcement Learning. Currently, his research interests span the areas of social network analysis, geometric deep learning and reinforcement learning.

He has been closely collaborating with various industrial research labs, such as Google Research, Intel Research, Ericsson R&D, TCS, Robert Bosch, KLA Tencor, Applied Materials, GE, Adobe Research, IBM India Research Labs, Yahoo! Labs and General Motors, working on applications of AI techniques to hard real-world problems. He also serves as a consultant to many startups in this space. He has also been quoted in top publications such as MIT Technology Review, Harvard Business Review, and Forbes India.

Mr. R. Sridhar (Founder-Managing Director) is an Electronics and Communications Engineer with 25+ years of experience in IT Infrastructure Industry, with a demonstrated record of setting up new business, exceeding the set goals of the business units, turning around underperforming units, driving increased revenue and market share.

He has a proven track record in driving operational excellence, revenue growth and people management. He has worked in various levels from executive to middle management, leadership roles in various organizations, viz., Schneider Electric India, Commscope, Tata InfoTech, and Accel ICIM (Fujitsu).

He played a pivotal role in setting up the first IP Broadband Network (MPLS) backbone for the country, and the Kerala State Data Center. He has a very good knowledge in Network Infrastructure solutions (ICT/ELV) and has executed high value critical network infra projects in manufacturing plants, Airports, Metro Rail, Railways and Telecom.

Prof. Nandan Sudarsanam (Founder-Director) is an Associate Professor in Department of Management Studies in IIT Madras and Core Group Member of Robert Bosch Centre for Data Science and AI. He is an advisor to Tamilnadu eGovernance Agency on emerging Technologies.

He has worked in Wall street before joining IITM. His area of interests include Experimentation, Machine learning/ Data Mining, Applied Statistics, Algorithmic and Heuristic approaches to problem solving.
