I am now with the University of Bonn, Germany, and with the Fraunhofer IAIS, Sankt Augustin, Germany. See my new hompage.

At CSAIL, I was a post-doc working with Prof. Leslie Pack Kaelbling. I got my PhD in Computer Science in April 2006 from the Albert-Ludwigs-University of Freiburg, Germany, working with Prof. Luc De Raedt. My main research interests are Statistical Relational Learning, Machine Learning and Data Mining in general, Computational Biology, and Robotics.
